Welcome to Betty Car

At Betty Car, we believe every journey should be safe, comfortable, and memorable. Founded in Bishoftu, Ethiopia, our passion is to make car rental easy and accessible for everyone—whether you're a local, a visitor, or a business traveler.

Our Story

Betty Car started with a simple idea: to provide reliable and affordable transportation for the Bishoftu community and beyond. Over the years, we've grown into a trusted name, known for our friendly service, well-maintained vehicles, and commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

To deliver top-quality car rental experiences with honesty, transparency, and a personal touch.

Our Vision

To become Ethiopia's most loved car rental brand, setting the standard for service and value.

Our Values

  • Customer First: We go the extra mile for your comfort and safety.
  • Integrity: Honest pricing and transparent service, always.
  • Reliability: Well-maintained cars, ready when you need them.
  • Community: Proudly serving Bishoftu and supporting local growth.

Our Journey

  • 2018: Betty Car is founded in Bishoftu with just 3 cars and a dream.
  • 2020: Expanded our fleet to 15 vehicles and introduced online booking.
  • 2022: Served our 1,000th customer and received the Bishoftu Service Award.
  • 2024: Now offering 10+ car types and serving all of Oromia region!

Frequently Asked Questions

    A: A valid driver’s license and a government-issued ID or passport.
  • Q: Can I book a car online?
    A: Yes! Use our website to browse cars and book instantly.
  • Q: Is there a minimum rental period?

    A: Our support team is available 24/7 to assist you.
