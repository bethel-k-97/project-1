Terms & Services

Please read our terms and conditions carefully before renting a vehicle from Betty Car. Our goal is to ensure a safe, fair, and enjoyable experience for all our customers.

Eligibility

  • All renters must present a valid driver's license and be at least 21 years old.

Rental Policy

  • Rental period is calculated on a 24-hour basis. Late returns may incur additional charges.
  • All vehicles must be returned in the same condition as rented. Damages will be charged accordingly.
  • No smoking or pets allowed in the vehicles.
  • Fuel policy: Vehicles must be returned with the same fuel level as at the start of the rental.

Payment & Insurance

  • Insurance is included, but the renter is responsible for any excess as per the agreement.
  • Payment is required in advance for the entire rental period.

Other

  • Betty Car reserves the right to refuse service to anyone at its discretion.

Need Help?

  • Q: What if I have questions about the terms?
    A: Contact our support team anytime at bettycargmail.com.
  • Q: Can I extend my rental?
    A: Yes, please contact us before your rental period ends to arrange an extension.
  • Q: What if my car breaks down?
    A: Call our 24/7 support line for immediate assistance. We’ll help you get back on the road quickly.
